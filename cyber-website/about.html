About Bizbit Solutions

Empowering Kenyan SMEs with complete tech & business solutions

Our Story

Bizbit Solutions was founded to help Kenyan SMEs thrive by making technology and business services accessible, affordable, and effective.

We know that small businesses face challenges with tech, compliance, and operations. From web design and M-Pesa integration to accounting, government services, and digital marketing, Bizbit Solutions is your one-stop partner for growth.

Our team supports you with tech consultancy, system development, data analytics, legal compliance, and more — so you can focus on your business.
